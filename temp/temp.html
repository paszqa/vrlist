10 Pin Bowling

we`ve all been down to our local bowling alley to play. But ended up having to wait for hours to get a lane. Don`t waste anymore more time, run the game up and play. No more waiting, just straight to the fun part. Multiplayer on one pc sharing your headset.

\n\nStuck in lockdown, Wanting to go bowling, or just looking for something to do.
\nTry our new Bowling Game With Full VR With Motion Controllers support.
\nDon`t Have a VR System, no worry\'s this bowling game also supports a standard display with keyboard & mouse or xbox 360/one controller

Game modes

10-Pin Bowling (Fully Implemented),

How To Play

Solo.
\nMultiplayer via VR headset sharing.

\n\nYou can play the game with The Following controller setups.
\nVR Headset with motion controllers (Tested HTC Vive),
\nPC Monitor With Xbox 360/One Controller Or
\nPC Monitor With keyboard & Mouse.

Built With Ue4.
